About LifeConnect

Connecting donors and recipients across India

🎯 Our Mission

LifeConnect was built to eliminate the gap between blood donors and people in need. Every year, thousands of lives are lost due to unavailability of blood at the right time. We aim to solve that with technology.

🛠 Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript
  • Backend: Python Flask
  • Database: MySQL (MySQL Workbench)
  • Security: Werkzeug password hashing, Flask sessions

✨ Features

  • User registration and secure login
  • Donor registration with blood group & location
  • Search donors by blood group and city
  • Post urgent blood requests
  • Dashboard to manage profile and requests
  • Toggle donor availability
